Panes & Layouts

Layout patterns and pane structures for dashboards and detail views. Use design tokens (--vw-space-*, --vw-radius-*) for consistent spacing and surfaces. This section will define pane entities, split layouts, and main/sidebar patterns.

1. Split pane (left / right)

Two panes side by side. List or filters on the left, main content on the right. Gaps and padding use --vw-space-*; borders use --borderColor.

Sidebar

Filters, nav, or list.

Main content

Detail view or primary content area.

Left sidebar, center content, right rail. Useful for dashboards or settings.
